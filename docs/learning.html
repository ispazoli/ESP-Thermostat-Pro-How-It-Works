Practical effects

Preheat

Without learning, the schedule time means “start heating at 07:00”. With learning, schedule time means “be warm at 07:00”. The controller uses heat-up rate to compute an optimal start time.

Stability

Heat loss estimation helps avoid overshoot/undershoot and makes the control smoother across weather changes. It also makes the system more predictable after big setpoint jumps.
